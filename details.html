DATES

The dates of MCPUCMUN’24 are 20th and 21st November 2024. Registrations are open from 25th October 2024. The last date for registration is on 15th November 2024. Students from Grade 8 up to Degree first year are eligible to participate.

ELIGIBILTY

Participation is open to students currently enrolled in the following academic levels:
1. Grade 8 to Grade 12 students of any recognised institution
2. First-year undergraduate students pursuing a bachelor’s degree in any field of study.

DRESS CODE

Day 1 - Formals (exception for AIPPM).
Day 2 - Ethnic wear for all committees.

COUNTRY ALLOTMENT

MCPUCMUN allows participants/institutions to present a list of their preferred committees and allotments, but the allotments will be done based on experience. The discretion of the Executive Board Members will prevail in all circumstances. We hope you respect our decisions regarding the same.
