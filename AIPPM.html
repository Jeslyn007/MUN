ALL INDIA POLITICAL PARTY MEET

AGENDA: THE ROLE OF POLITICAL PARTIES IN ACHIEVING UNIVERSAL EDUCATION FOR THE GIRL CHILD BY 2030

All India Political Party Meets are conventions that allow the diverse political groups of India to reach a consensus on decisions of national importance, providing a platform for unrestrained political debate, discussion, and deliberation. The duty of each representative in this committee is to uphold the true spirit of democracy while also keeping their political interests in mind. The main purpose of this is to ensure that all the participating members come down to consensual solutions with respect to the issues at hand.

In this session, our discussion shall primarily focus on the subject that holds earnest attention in the recent times "The role of political parties in achieving universal education for the girl child by 2030" Reaffirming the principles of equality and for the pursuit of our nation's prosperity we look forward towards strategic discussions, innovative and beneficial solutions which will in turn help empower and improve the status of women in India. Taking into account, the importance of education in the modern world it should be accessible to all, regardless of gender or other social divisions.
