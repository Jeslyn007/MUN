ABOUT US

“Public Speaking has always been the weapon one wields to leave a legacy in the minds of the audience.”

MCPUCMUN fosters global awareness, enhances research and public speaking skills. It also promotes diplomacy and cultural exchange. Furthermore, it does encourage civic engagement; simulates real-world negotiation. The MCPUCMUN’24 also strives to support personal and professional development. Students can explore innovative and original solutions to real world problems. The youth can tackle and voice their opinions on a platform. This will encourage and help them foster their inner world leaders.

MCPUCMUN, an emerging Model United Nations in the Bangalore MUN circuit, aims to give the delegates a memorable and fruitful experience. With MCPUCMUN’24 being the second edition, we intend to create another delightful experience to all delegates. MCPUCMUN’24 fosters teamwork and cultural awareness, allowing students to understand diverse perspectives and complex global challenges, ultimately preparing them for future leadership roles.
