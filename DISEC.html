DISARMAMENT AND INTERNATIONAL SECURITY COMMITTEE

AGENDA: ROLE OF SEMICONDUCTOR MICROCHIPS IN AWS

The Disarmament and International Security Committee (DISEC) ,formally known as the First Committee of the United Nations General Assembly, is where delegates come together to discuss global security challenges, arms control, and disarmament efforts. To collaborate and propose solutions on issues like nuclear non-proliferation, arms trafficking, and conflict resolution. DISEC is essential in promoting international peace and fostering diplomacy in the face of global security concerns, giving participants a realistic experience of multilateral negotiations.

In this session, our focus remains on an issue that holds profound importance for the world semiconductor microchips are not just a technical component of autonomous weapon systems; they are a pivotal element in global military, economic, and geopolitical landscapes. Their influence spans from enhancing the operational capabilities of AWS to driving ethical discussions and shaping international relations. As the world continues to rely on advanced technologies for both civilian and military purposes, the role of semiconductor microchips in AWS will remain a critical factor in determining global power structures, security frameworks, and the future of warfare.
